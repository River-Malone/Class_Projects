What Is Shaky Knees?

Shaky Knees is a popular annual music festival held in Atlanta, Georgia, known for showcasing a diverse lineup of indie rock, alternative, and emerging artists. In the 12 years since its founding, the festival has evolved into a major event in the U.S. music scene. Unlike many other large festivals that focus on flashy gimmicks, Shaky Knees prioritizes the music, offering a laid-back yet energetic atmosphere. Over the course of three days, the festival features multiple stages and hosts performances by legendary bands and up-and-coming acts. While it's usually held in Central Park for 2025, the festival changed venues to Peidmont park due to Motown Midtown being on hiatus.
